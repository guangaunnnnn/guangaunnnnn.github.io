《築東廠域Bake to square one :Jhu Dong》

By Guan 02 September 2016

National Taipei University of Technology Graduation Project , 2016

基地:台灣新竹,竹東 關鍵詞:地方上的記憶,附掛式,支配性地景

Location: Jhu Dong,HsinChu city,Taiwan Keywords: Sense of place/ Cultural heritage /Museum/ Plug-in architecture

介紹 Introduction

新竹縣竹東鎮的台灣水泥廠，曾有一段時間，這片土地是以「台泥竹東廠」的空間形式，成為地方主要產業。

There was a time that Taiwan Cement Factory in Jhu-Dong Township, HsinChu County, proved to be a major local industry, and this land used the space formation of Taiwan Cement Jhu-Dong Factory. The cement factory was primitively rough, with industrial architecture by a great scale, and it also forms a dominant landscape. In the factory, the operating chimney of eight stories high was a representative landscape in Jhu-Dong as well.

水泥廠原始粗曠、超大尺度的工業建築型態，也成為重要支配性地景，廠區內的八樓高作業煙囪也是竹東的代表性地景。當土地由田地變工廠，現在又要變成住宅區、商業區，「土地的價值越來越高，但土地與在地居民關係卻越來越遠」。

When the land turned into factories from paddy fields, and now it is going to become residential areas and business districts, but “the higher land values, the farther relationship between land and local residents will be”.

居民認同物消失，一部份居民離開，使地區居民連結日益鬆散－以重塑消失的在地物：找回居民對土地的認同、竹東人與外來訪客可以共享的場所，使之成為鄰近社區生活息息相關；是社區資料庫；是休閒與教育場所，也是社區象徵，並植入當地的特色對抗土地開發的資本邏輯。 As residents’ identification with the land fades away, part of them leave, and this makes locals’ connection loose day by day. What reform fading local surroundings are local archives, a recreational and educational space as well as a community symbol; it helps residents find their identification with the land back, and embeds local specialties against capital logic of land development. The local archive is not only a place that Jhu-Dong residents and visitors all share but a way to make neighborhood communities closely bound up.

讓當地興盛的起點，以地方文化元素、對於地方感的想像，在竹東台泥與竹東人間為依循投射。 It is a starting point of reviving the local which interacts with Jhu-Dong Taiwan Cement Corporation (TCC) and Jhu-Dong locals, through its cultural elements and imagination based on local impression.
